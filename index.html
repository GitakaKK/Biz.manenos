Introducing the ultimate mobile app
for managing your business using your phone

Biz Manenos is the future, through our platform you will be able to monitor and observe operational metrics for your business all from the convinience of your mobile device.

Speed of Access.

Get fast access to your data straight from the mobile app. We understand that businesses are in constant need of past records and other business information during decision making. Through our infrastructure, clients need not to worry about the time it takes to access this vital information.

Instant graphs and statistical data.

Through our quantitative tools and measurement analytics we are able to provide real time tables and data charts to help our clients make informed decisions about key points about the business. We go further by make it possible to share this graphical data in real time.

It's all in the Cloud.

Data storage can be an issue when dealing with large data sets of company information. We make the process of storing and managing this data for our clients as simple as possble. We understand that different clients have different storage needs and that is why we are commited to ensure that we have the capacity to provide for both heavy and light usage.

Security & Privacy.

With the ever growing threat from cybersecurity both as an external and internal factor in business, we have dedicated an entire division of security experts and analysts to ensure that we are providing the latest in technology and security advancement to our clients. We ensure that the privacy and security of their data is our most important priority.

How Does It Work

Im sure you are wondering how does the platform achieve all of these features. Well we are using the latest in crypto analytic methodology by peering users and data in a server network.
